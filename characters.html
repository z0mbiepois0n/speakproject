Mr. Freeman

Mr. Freeman is the art teacher at Melindas school. Despite her struggling everywhere else she does well in art. In her school Mr. Freeman is one of the few teachers Melinda tolerates. Despite her silence her and Mr. Freeman have a type of understanding. Mr. Freeman is one of the supportive characters in the novel to Melinda.

Melinda

Melinda is the protaginist of the book. Shes a freshman and is already disliked by her classmates after called the cops at a party after being assulted. She's hardly ever speaks due to her truama. She struggles to find herself and friends with her issolation from her peers. Through out the book we see her grow and overcome her truama and make friends.

IT

IT is the person who assulted Melinda. He is unfortatly an important charcter as her is the reason that lead to the place Melinda is. Fortantanly he is not mentioned often. Melinda struggles to reveal the truth about the vile creature IT is.

Racheal

Racheal is Melinda ex best-friend who stops being Melindas friend after the party. She decides she hates Melinda and doesn't speak with her and ignores her even when Melinda is alone because of no one wanting to talk to her.

Heather

Heather is one of Melinda few friends after most the school disliked Melinda. Having just moved to the city she doesn't know anyone and is desperate to get into populatity while Melinda doesn't want to. With their conflicting prioities and other things Melinda learns to find people to be friend who are the right people and are real friends.

Ivy

Ivy is one Melindas friends at her school. They befriend each other through their art class. Despite them not being more than acquaintances at the start become friends throughout the book.
