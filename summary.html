Summary

Speak is a novel about Melinda Sordino a freshman in High School. After being assulted at a party the summer before the year started, she called the cops causing the party to end and subsequently causing her classmates to dislike her leaving her without friends and isolated. Throughout the book Melinda must learn to gain the strength to speak about what happened to her. She also must learn to find true friends and how to find a way to express herself even if it isn't vocally. Melinda must find a way to find herself as a young teenage while dealing with her truama and social isolation.
