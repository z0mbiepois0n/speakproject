Review

Overall, I enjoyed this book a lot. I would give it a 4/5 stars. The writing styles may seem odd and not be enjoyable to some people. As mentioned, this book contains sensitive subjects such as SA, so if that's a topic you don't feel comfortable reading about I recommend you skip this book though nothing is graphically stated. It's a very interesting book with both sad and heartwarming topics. Overall I think this is a good book and do suggest it to anyone who wishes to read it.
